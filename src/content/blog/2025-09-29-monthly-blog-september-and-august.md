Whoa a blog post!

I was really busy last month and completely forgot about writing a blog post until it was halfway through September…

## What was I busy with?

I moved house! I'm now living 4 hours drive away from where I used to and also living with my wonderful partner! I've only just settled into a schedule here and I've been learning how to have someone else in my space since I've been by myself for 5 years. Kinda hectic and confusing but I'm getting the hang of it!

I've also been resting and relaxing a lot more. I'm very much a workaholic and I've been burned out for a long time, so I've been putting in more effort into not making everything about work and finding the thread in my creativity that I've lost.

Speaking of:

We finished that short film! Unfortunately, it's not been uploaded anywhere and the google link I had to it is dead… We struggled to get it done by the deadline, so it still needed a bit more polish, but we made it and I'm happy with making something!

Aside from that, I've only just gotten back to uni after 4 weeks, I don't actually have a lot on my plate this trimester since I've chosen to delay the really big class/project and focus on my internship.

I'm currently working with **StarsTV in Sydney**! The project I'm working on specifically is a secret (shh), but once it's done, I'll make sure it goes here. I'm not letting this one get away!

I'm also working on more branding and portfolio stuff! I'm at the point at uni where graduation is getting close and I've got to sit down and think "What do I actually want to do as a career?"

I do a LOT when it comes to being creative, so this has been tough to work out, but for now I want to focus on **environment design** as a career. It's not something I thought I would enjoy, but every time I've had to design a location for an assignment, I've been really into it. It also ties 2D and 3D art together, which works perfect for me!

Of course, this does cause a slight problem with the fact I barely have any environment work on my portfolio. I've been working on personal projects to put up, so get excited for that!

## What have I been…

- **Playing:** TOEM, In Stars And Time. I've been on a biiiiig monochromatic game trip lately. TOEM is such a delightful game, and I'm so hyped for the sequel, so fun to play. *In Stars And Time* is a game that means so incredibly much to me and I've only just finished it — I'm a sucker for time loops.
- **Reading:** *Wotakoi: Love Is Hard For Otaku*, *How Do We Relationship*. I fell out of reading HARD, so I wanted to reread Wotakoi since I love catching new references since I first read it. *How Do We Relationship* came from me realizing that despite how much I love yuri, I haven't read a whole lot of it, and I'm fixing it!! Very cute so far, only on volume 1 but I will be getting more!
