Welcome! To the blog! I'll be posting one of these every month to talk about what I've been doing both in projects and in my spare time!

First up, the big thing has been making this website! I've never actually made a portfolio before aside from my ArtStation and even then I don't post there unless I'm trying to collate a bunch of stuff together. I hadn't actually realized how much work I'd made until I was putting it all on here and WOW I do a lot. Honestly, I got to feel really good about my progression as an artist this month.

The blog itself is also a big thing this month! I wanted to do this partly for marketing but also it forces me to sit down and acknowledge all the stuff I've done in a month, which is a nice little break from just constantly working.

## What can I share progress wise?

I've been working on a virtual production for the first time! I'm the production manager as well as the 3d animator AND live actor, so a lot has been going on. I've never done an acting role aside from voice acting before, normally I'm behind the camera so it's been a really different experience. I've also discovered that I'm very giggly and I keep smiling. I am not in a role where I should be smiling. I have dance training where you're supposed to smile ALL THE TIME, this is weird! Hopefully we get a take where I'm not smiling before this production wraps!

As for the side that I'm actually more confident in, here's some of the animations that I've made for our Devil character!

## What have I learned?

Most of this trimester has been a lot of prepping for going into industry and what isn't that has just been reinforcing skills I've already learned! We've nearly finished the trimester so things are starting to ramp up a bit, the goal is get through it without overworking myself but we'll see!
